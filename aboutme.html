About Chang Lin

Introduction

Hello, my name is Chang Lin and I’m a 19-year-old sophomore majoring journalism and mass communication, communication arts, and pursuing a certificate in business-marketing at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Originally from China. As a journalism major, I’m passionate about writing, reporting, and storytelling, and I’m eager to develop my skills and explore new forms of media.

I’m an extraverted person who loves to explore new places, try new foods, and meet new people. In my free time, I enjoy cooking, experimenting with new recipes, catching a movie, listening to musicals, or working on my film production skills. I find inspiration in the creative ways these mediums can connect people and tell compelling stories. My favorite movie of all time is “Titanic” and I’m constantly inspired by the way it tells a compelling story and brings people together.
